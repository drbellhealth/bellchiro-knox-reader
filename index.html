About the Practice

Bell Family Chiropractic is a long-running family-oriented chiropractic office in West Knoxville. The practice offers chiropractic adjustments, spinal decompression therapy, soft-tissue work, posture/ergonomic assessment, and conservative care for the common musculoskeletal complaints — lower back pain, neck pain, sciatica, headaches, and recovery from auto or sports injuries.
