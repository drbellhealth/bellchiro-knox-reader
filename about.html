About Knoxville Chiropractor Reader

An independent local-information site about chiropractic care in Knoxville, Tennessee, with a focus on Bell Family Chiropractic on Sherlake Lane.

What This Site Is

This site exists to help people in the Knoxville area get a clear picture of what a chiropractic visit looks like before they call — what's offered, what's typically treated, what to expect on the first visit, and how the practice we cover (Bell Family Chiropractic) fits into the local landscape. The information here is general and educational; it isn't a substitute for an in-person evaluation.

About the Practice We Cover

Bell Family Chiropractic is a long-running family-oriented chiropractic office in West Knoxville. The practice offers chiropractic adjustments, spinal decompression therapy, soft-tissue work, posture/ergonomic assessment, and conservative care for the common musculoskeletal complaints — lower back pain, neck pain, sciatica, headaches, and recovery from auto or sports injuries. The office is located at 111 Sherlake Lane, Suite 101, Knoxville, TN 37922, and the main number is +1 865-383-7730.

How We Maintain Independence

This site is editorial — not the official website of the practice. We collect publicly available information, summarize it in plain language, and link to the practice for booking and contact. We don't accept advertising and we don't take payment from the practice for coverage. For booking, billing, insurance verification, or any clinical question, contact the office directly.
